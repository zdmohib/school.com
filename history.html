History and influence

The school was established in 1965 and follows the traditional Secondary School Certificate (SSC) curriculum with the medium of teaching being Bangla. There is intense competition in the admission process and large proportions of the applicants are rejected. The admission process consists of both written and oral examination. Many of the graduates from the school become leaders and notable personnel of the country. It is one of the most prestigious schools in the country, despite having logistical problems. Most graduates from here do well in other areas of life.
