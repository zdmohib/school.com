About

The Dhanmondi Government Boys'​ High School is one of the elite public boys'​ schools in Bangladesh. It was established in 1965 in East Pakistan, now Bangladesh, the school enrols students from year 1 to year 10. Every year about 300 students appear in examinations from Science and Commerce group.
